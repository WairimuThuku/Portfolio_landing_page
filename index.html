About Me

Growing up near the airport and with the exposure of aircrafts, i developed an interest in the aviation field! After high school I pursued pilot training at the Wilson Airport and graduated with a PPL(private pilot licence) I later joined JKUAT university for my degree in Information Technology but later dropped since i was not seeing value for my money. Along side that I've worked at a baby shop as a sales person, I've also worked with a private company in fixing CCTVs and laptops, I've also styled and sold mens wear.
